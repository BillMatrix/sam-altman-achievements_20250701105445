About Sam Altman

Sam Altman is a prominent American entrepreneur, investor, and technology executive. Currently serving as the CEO of OpenAI, he is at the forefront of artificial intelligence research and development. Prior to his role at OpenAI, Altman was the president of Y Combinator, one of the world's most influential startup accelerators.

Known for his visionary thinking and strategic investments, Altman has played a pivotal role in shaping the technology landscape. His insights on entrepreneurship, technology trends, and the future of AI have made him a respected voice in the industry.

Throughout his career, Altman has demonstrated a commitment to advancing technology that benefits humanity while being mindful of potential risks, particularly in the field of artificial intelligence.

Key Roles

CEO of OpenAI

As CEO of OpenAI, Sam leads the organization's mission to ensure that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity. Under his leadership, OpenAI has developed groundbreaking AI systems like GPT-4 and DALL-E.

Former President of Y Combinator

From 2014 to 2019, Sam served as the president of Y Combinator, where he helped hundreds of startups grow and succeed. During his tenure, YC expanded its reach and influence in the startup ecosystem.
