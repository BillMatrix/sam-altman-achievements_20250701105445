Birth and Upbringing

Sam Altman was born on April 22, 1985, in Chicago, Illinois. He spent his formative years growing up in St. Louis, Missouri, where he would begin to develop the skills and interests that would later define his career.

Did You Know?

St. Louis, where Sam grew up, has a rich history of innovation and has produced several notable tech entrepreneurs.

Family

Sam comes from a professional family background:

  • His mother worked as a dermatologist
  • His father was a real estate broker
  • Sam is the eldest of four siblings

This family environment likely contributed to his drive and professional outlook from an early age.

Schooling

Sam received his primary and secondary education at John Burroughs School, a prestigious private school located in Ladue, Missouri. This institution is known for its rigorous academic standards and has produced several notable alumni.

At John Burroughs School, Sam likely developed the foundation of knowledge and skills that would later help him succeed in the tech industry and as an entrepreneur.

University Education

Sam's higher education journey was unconventional but impactful:

  • Attended Stanford University to study computer science
  • Completed two years before dropping out in 2005 to pursue entrepreneurial ventures
  • Received an honorary degree from the University of Waterloo in 2017, recognizing his contributions to technology and business

This path reflects a pattern seen with many successful tech entrepreneurs who prioritize hands-on experience and real-world application over traditional educational completion.
